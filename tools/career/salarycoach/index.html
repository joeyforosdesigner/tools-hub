How to Use SalaryCoach

Negotiating a salary can be one of the most stressful aspects of the hiring process, but it doesn't have to be. SalaryCoach is designed to bridge the gap between receiving a job offer and accepting a package that truly reflects your worth. To get started, simply copy the text of your job offer letter—including the base salary, signing bonus, equity options, and benefits—and paste it into the input field above.

Once your offer is pasted, use the sidebar controls to customize the AI's approach. If you are naturally reserved or want to maintain a very friendly relationship with your future employer, select the "Polite" tone. If you want to project strength and firmness, choose "Assertive." You can also adjust the confidence slider to reflect how comfortable you feel with negotiation tactics; this helps the AI tailor the complexity of the arguments.

Click the "Analyze Offer" button to initiate the process. The tool will cross-reference your offer against current market data for similar roles, identifying any discrepancies between what you were offered and what the market dictates. Within seconds, you will receive a detailed breakdown including a specific counter-offer number, a gap analysis, and a word-for-word script designed to help you articulate your value effectively. Finally, use the "Save PDF" button to download your strategy, allowing you to rehearse the script before your call or email.

Frequently Asked Questions

How do I negotiate salary without being rude?

Negotiation is a standard business practice, not a personal conflict. Express gratitude for the offer first, then frame your request around market value and your unique skills. SalaryCoach generates scripts that maintain a professional and respectful tone while still advocating for your interests.

What is a reasonable counter-offer percentage?

While it varies by industry, a counter-offer of 10% to 20% above the initial base salary is generally considered reasonable. Our AI analyzes specific market data for your role to suggest a precise number rather than a generic percentage.

Should I negotiate benefits if the salary is fixed?

Absolutely. If the base salary is non-negotiable, you can often negotiate other components such as signing bonuses, vacation time, remote work flexibility, or equity grants. The tool identifies leverage points in benefits as well.

Can I use this tool for a promotion negotiation?

Yes. While designed for job offers, you can paste your current role description and market data for your target title to generate a script for asking for a raise or promotion during a performance review.

How accurate is the market data used by the AI?

The AI utilizes up-to-date compensation trends and benchmarks available in its training data, combined with real-time analysis of the specific details in your offer to provide a highly relevant estimate.

What if the employer rescinds the offer because I negotiated?

This is extremely rare and usually a red flag about the company culture. Professional employers expect candidates to negotiate. Using a polite and data-backed approach, like the ones generated here, minimizes any risk of offense.

Is my job offer data stored or shared?

No. Your job offer text is processed solely for the purpose of generating your negotiation strategy. We do not store your personal employment data on our servers.

How do I deliver the negotiation script?

You can deliver the script via email or during a phone call. If over the phone, practice reading it aloud until it sounds natural rather than robotic. The tool allows you to adjust the tone to match your speaking style.
